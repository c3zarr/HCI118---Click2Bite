About Click2Bite

Click2Bite is a simple and easy ordering system that helps students and staff order food from the school canteen more easily. It reduces long lines, saves time, and makes getting food quicker and more convenient. With the app, users can check the menu and order their food instantly.

How It Works

1. Log In

Sign in securely using your WMSU school account credentials.

2. Browse & Order

Browse menus from different canteens and add your favorites to the cart.

3. Get Notified

Relax! Receive a notification on your phone when your order is ready.

4. Pick Up

Head to the pickup counter, show your order confirmation, and enjoy!
